# Superstore-Sales-Analysis-Power-BI-
BI
🔍 Objective

To analyze Superstore sales and profit trends across 4 years and identify the top 5 selling and profitable sub-categories.

🛠 Tools & Techniques

Power Query Editor – for data cleaning & transformation

DAX Measures – to calculate KPIs (Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount, Profit Margin %)

Power BI Visualizations – cards, clustered bar chart, line chart, table, slicers & filters

📊 Key Insights

📈 Overall sales & profit growth trend across years

🏆 Top 5 sub-categories contributing maximum sales & profit

🌍 Regional & segment-wise performance comparison

🔎 Profit margin analysis with discount patterns
